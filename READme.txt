In the trades pre-processing folder, you will find scripts that helped clean the trade data and produce Trades.py.

In the training folder, you will find scripts that were used during the training of different models.

In the variable pre-processing folder, you will find scripts that helped prepare additional variables for merging with the trades dataframe.

The FullGermanModelWithLag.csv and FullGermanModelWithLag.py is essentially the dataset used for all training models, but sometimes some of the variables have been removed before training the model. The csv file is the product of the script.

The ARIMA.py and Test.py are the scripts used for making and training the ARIMA model and testing the final TFT model on the test set, respectively.
